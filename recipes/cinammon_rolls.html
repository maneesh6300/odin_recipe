best cinammon rolls dish

Make the night before, rolls rise in the fridge overnight and bake the next morning. Easy and delicious. Enjoy

  • ⅓ cup butter
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 4 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 eggs
